    • Applying Fix ip address:-

  • Goto: Search bar and type: Advanced Network Configuration ENTER
  • Select your internet connection and click on Gear icon.
  • Goto: IPv4 Settings/Add
    Address: 192.168.0.100
    Netmask: 24
    Gateway: 192.168.0.255 /Save

  • Now goto the Terminal window and fire these commands:
  • ifconfig eth0 down ENTER
  • service networking restart ENTER
  • ifconfig eth0 up ENTER
  • ifconfig eth0 ENTER
